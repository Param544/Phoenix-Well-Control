üíº Course Offerings

Our specialized training programs are designed for non-drilling personnel, contractors, and support teams who work on oil & gas rig sites. Safety starts with awareness ‚Äî and we prepare you to act when it counts.

1. Well Control Basics for Non-Drilling Personnel

Target Audience: Mud loggers, wireline crew, mud engineers, service company reps, safety officers, trainees, and housekeeping staff.

  • Rig overview & roles
  • Pressure basics
  • Kick indicators
  • Emergency actions
  • Communication protocols

Duration: 1-2 days | Certificate: Awareness (2 years)

2. Rig Site Safety Orientation

Target Audience: Visitors or temporary personnel entering the red zone.

  • Rig layout & risk zones
  • PPE & behavior standards
  • Safety signs & alarms
  • Emergency drills

Duration: Half-day | Certificate: Safety Badge (1 year)

3. Introduction to Managed Pressure Drilling (MPD)

Target Audience: Service engineers, HSE staff, and trainees.

  • MPD components & objectives
  • Pressure control basics
  • Integration with well control
  • Risk recognition

Duration: 1 day | Certificate: Completion

4. Human Factors in Well Control

Target Audience: All rig personnel.

  • Situational awareness
  • Decision fatigue
  • Effective communication
  • Case studies & role-play

Duration: 1 day | Format: Workshops

5. Hazard Identification & Barrier Thinking

Target Audience: Non-drilling & support roles.

  • Bowtie diagrams
  • Barrier degradation
  • Risk mitigation
  • Job stoppage awareness

Duration: 1 day | Format: Workshop

6. Blowout Preventer (BOP) Basics

Target Audience: Non-certified personnel near BOP systems.

  • BOP stack basics
  • Role during kicks
  • Accident prevention

Duration: Half-day | Format: Online / Instructor-led
